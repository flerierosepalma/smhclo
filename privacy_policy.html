SMH's Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make a purchase from SMH CLO.

Personal Information

When you visit the SMH CLO, we automatically collect certain information about your device, particularly your Device Information and Order Information.

As you browse at SMH CLO, we collect information about the individual web pages or products that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the site, and information about how you interact with the site. We refer to this automatically collected information as Device Information.

Additionally, when you make a purchase or attempt to make a purchase through the Site, we collect certain information from you, including your name, billing address, shipping address, payment information (including credit card numbers, gacsh/paymaya phone numbers, email address, and phone number. This is called Order Information.


Children's Information

We believe it is important to provide added protection for children online. We encourage parents and guardians to spend time online with their children to observe, participate in and/or monitor and guide their online activity. SMH CLO does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian believes that SMH CLO has in its database the personally-identifiable information of a child under the age of 13, please contact us immediately (using the contact in the first paragraph) and we will use our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.


Online Privacy Policy Only

This privacy policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website and regarding information shared and/or collected there.

This policy does not apply to any information collected offline or via channels other than this website.


Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our privacy policy and agree to its terms.
